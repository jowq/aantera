Use Cases

Is RPA the silver bullet

As much interest RPA has received over the past year due to a sudden spike in working from home arrangements, it isn’t a be-all, end-all software to rule them all. However, RPA’s increasing popularity and adoption is predicated on its cost effective utility and application across industries relative to the traditional clunky, made-to-measure software requiring on-the-clock tech support and costly annual licensing fee. Below are some examples on how RPA can benefit various functions within an organisation.

Accounting and Finance

The application of RPA in accounting and finance operations is one of the best use case for enterprises due to its rigid, rules-based requirement. 

Manual quote generation, invoice processing and managing reconciliations will be a thing of the past as RPA, coupled with Optical Character Recognition (OCR), will be able to cross-check and automate various touch points within the process, saving time while eliminating mistakes. 

With minimal human interaction and zero errors, RPA strengthens processes while the company benefits from higher assurance and smoother audit exercise.

Human Resource

HR professionals can be innundated with performing data entry on multiple software; a commonplace for many undergoing multi-year digital transformation from legacy ERP systems to cloud-based SaaS.

RPA can automate data entry processes, replicating and entering data across various software in a fraction of time, reclaiming up to 40% of a working professional’s time to focus on recruitment and managing corporate talent. 

Customer Service

Customer Service/Success (CS) roles are often multitasking and on the clock to meet and exceed KPIs thus increasing the probability of errors and deviation from standard operating procedure (SOP).

RPA can reduce transaction times by automating dialers, updating CRMs, expand automated communication to allow CS representatives to focus on delivering the message while fostering better relationships with customers and clients alike.
